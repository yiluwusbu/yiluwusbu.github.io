Bio

I'm a final year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Dongyoon Lee. My research interest lies in building efficient and reliable computer systems, using solutions across the entire system stack: Operating Systems, Compilers and Computer Architecture. My current research focus on designing energy effiient crash-consistent Operating Systems and Compilers for battery-less intermittent computing.
